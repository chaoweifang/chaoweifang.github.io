Chaowei Fang

Biography

Chaowei FANG is an associate professor at School of Artificial Intelligence, Xidian University, and also a member of the Key Laboratory of Intelligent Perception and Image Understanding of Ministry of Education. He received Ph.D. degree from The University of Hong Kong in 2019, and B.E. degree from Xi'an Jiaotong University in 2013. His research interest includes visual information processing, representation, and understanding.

方超伟是西安电子科技大学人工智能学院副教授，智能感知与图像理解教育部重点实验室成员。2019年获得香港大学博士学位, 2013年获得西安交通大学学士学位。研究兴趣包括：视觉信息处理、表示与理解。
