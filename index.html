Chaowei Fang

Biography

Chaowei FANG is a associate professor at School of Artificial Intelligence, Xidian University. He is also a member of the Key Laboratory of Intelligent Perception and Image Understanding of Ministry of Education. He received Ph.d. degree (supervised by Prof. Yizhou Yu) from the Department of Computer Science, The University of Hong Kong in 2019, and received B.E. degree from Automation, Xi'an Jiaotong University in 2013. During 2013-2015, he was a postgraduate student at the Institute of Artificial Intelligence and Robotics, Xi'an Jiaotong University (supervised by Prof. Jianru Xue). His research interest mainly includes visual information processing, representation, and understanding. Here is his resume.
